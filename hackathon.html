How should you behave in social situations?

Obviously, a world after the pandemic is going to change your outdoor habits - whether you like it or not. And it is easy to get attached to our old norm of living. How should we change then? Take this short simulation to see how well you fare on living in the new world.

You wake up early in the morning and decided to go to the gym for a workout. You need to use gym weights for workout but you didn't bring any. What will you do?

Rent one from the service counter.
Borrow one from your friend and sanitize it.
Head home and bring yours.

Correct! It is safer to bring your own weights, as gym equipments can't be sanitized. Harsh chemicals found in sanitisers can deteriorate rubber and metals, both widely used in gym equipments.

You are hiring a worker to repair your spoilt washing machine. What are the precautions that is WRONG to follow during the service?

Avoid physical greeting
Maintain a safe distance with the service provider
Close the windows

Correct! You should open the windows for maximum ventilation. In the post-Covid world, it is important to adapt to the new norm, where you want to avoid physical greeting and maintain social distancing at all times, even after the pandemic.

You are planning to host a gathering and you need to decide an activity for the kids to play outdoor. Which activity will you choose?

Frisbee
Rope skipping
A game of tag

Correct! Both tag and rope skipping together means frequent direct contact, which is not ideal even after the pandemic. Frisbee can reduce the amount of direct contact among the children, thus reducing germs transmission.
